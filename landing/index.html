How it works

Four steps, all on your machine.

Watches

Sits in the tray and notices new files landing in Downloads (or any folder you pick).

Reads

Opens each file and extracts its text — including PDFs and OCR for images.

Decides

A local AI model picks the right category and a clean, descriptive new name.

Files it

Moves & renames it, notifies you, and remembers any correction you make.

🔒 Your documents never leave your PC

The AI runs locally. No servers, no accounts, no telemetry — invoices, contracts and IDs stay yours.
