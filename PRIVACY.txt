AI File Butler — Privacy Statement
Last updated: 2026-07

Short version: everything happens on your computer. AI File Butler does not
collect, send, or upload anything. There are no accounts, no servers, no
telemetry, and no tracking. It works fully offline.

WHAT THE APP DOES WITH YOUR DATA
- It reads the files in the folders you choose to watch, in order to sort and
  rename them. All reading happens on your device.
- For PDFs and scans it extracts text locally (Windows OCR). For photos it reads
  EXIF data (date, GPS) and, if you enable it, recognises FACES on-device.
- For music it reads the embedded tags.
- Optional AI naming: the file's name and a short text snippet are sent to a
  local AI model on your own machine via Ollama (http://localhost). This never
  leaves your computer and needs no internet.
- Optional face recognition: if you enroll people (a name + example photos), the
  app computes a numeric "face embedding" for each face and matches new photos to
  the closest enrolled person, on-device. Face embeddings are biometric data —
  they are stored only on your computer and are never uploaded.
- Optional semantic search: to let you find files in plain language, the app
  stores a short text snippet and a numeric embedding of each indexed document in
  a local index. This index contains text extracted from YOUR documents, so treat
  it as sensitive; it lives only on your device and you can delete it (see below).

WHAT IS STORED ON YOUR DEVICE (and nowhere else)
Everything lives under:  %LOCALAPPDATA%\AI File Butler\
  - butler_config.json  — your settings (watched folders, language, options).
  - learned_rules.json  — patterns learned from your corrections.
  - placements.json     — a record of where files were moved (to detect corrections).
  - undo_log.jsonl      — so moves can be undone.
  - reminders.json      — document/task expiry reminders you add or that are read.
  - people.json         — enrolled people and their face embeddings (BIOMETRIC data).
  - search_index.json   — text snippets + embeddings for semantic search.
  - models\             — the optional face-recognition model, downloaded on demand.
You can delete any of these files at any time to reset that feature, or delete the
whole folder to wipe everything the app knows about you.

WHAT IS NEVER DONE
- No file contents, file names, metadata, face data, or search text are uploaded
  anywhere.
- No analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or profiling.
- No internet connection is required for the app to work.

THIRD PARTIES
- Ollama and the AI/embedding models run locally on your device; the app does not
  send your data to Ollama's or anyone else's servers.
- If YOU choose to download the app or a model, those downloads go directly to the
  respective project's site (e.g. ollama.com) under their own terms.
