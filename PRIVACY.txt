AI File Butler — Privacy Statement
Last updated: 2026

Short version: everything happens on your computer. AI File Butler does not
collect, send, or upload anything. There are no accounts, no servers, no
telemetry, and no tracking.

WHAT THE APP DOES WITH YOUR DATA
- It reads the files in the folders you choose to watch, in order to sort and
  rename them. This reading happens entirely on your device.
- For PDFs it extracts text locally; for photos it reads EXIF data (date, GPS)
  and can run face DETECTION locally (it does not identify who anyone is); for
  music it reads the embedded tags.
- When the optional AI mode is enabled, the file's name and a short text snippet
  are sent to a local AI model running on your own machine via Ollama
  (http://localhost). This never leaves your computer and requires no internet.

WHAT IS STORED ON YOUR DEVICE (and nowhere else)
- butler_config.json   — your settings (watched folders, language, options).
- learned_rules.json   — patterns learned from your corrections.
- placements.json      — a record of where files were moved (to detect your
                         corrections).
- undo_log.jsonl       — so moves can be undone.
These are plain files next to the app. Delete them any time to reset the app.

WHAT IS NEVER DONE
- No file contents, file names, or metadata are uploaded anywhere.
- No analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or profiling.
- No internet connection is required for the app to work.

THIRD PARTIES
- Ollama and the AI models run locally on your device; the app does not send your
  data to Ollama's or anyone else's servers.
- If YOU choose to download the app or a model, those downloads go directly to
  the respective project's site (e.g. ollama.com) under their own terms.
